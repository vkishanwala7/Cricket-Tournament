Tournament Rules

  1. Overs: The match will consist of 5 overs per inning.
  2. Group Stage Bowling: One player can bowl a maximum of 2 overs; others only 1 over each.
  3. Knockout Bowling: Two players can bowl 2 overs; others only 1 over each.
  4. Player Participation: Every player in the team must bowl 1 over in the first 2 group matches. Else, team is disqualified from knockouts.
  5. Illegal Deliveries (Wide ball + No ball + Dead ball):
    • 6 illegal balls during an over will result in bowler replacement.
    • Bowler who bowled 6 illegal deliveries will not be counted for (Rule 4)
    • Replacement can finish the over; over is counted to replaced player.
    • If 5 legal balls are bowled before 6 illegal ones, rule may be skipped.
  6. Extras: 1 run for wide/no ball. Byes & overthrows count. Free hit on no ball (only run-out allowed).
  7. Bowling Type: Only slow/medium pace with 1 step allowed. Fast pace or more than 1 step = dead ball.
  8. Bonus for Girls, Ladies & U13 Boys: 1 run for side net hit (no bounce). Strike unchanged.
  9. Time Penalty: Next batter must come in under 1 min after wicket. Else, 5-run penalty.
  10. Upper Net Rule: Catch off the upper net is valid if taken directly.
